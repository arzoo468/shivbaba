The NAME is

shiv

He is called Shiv.

It is a name given to Him by his children based on his nature, his personality, his act and his virtues. The word Shiv means benefactor-one who works for everyone's welfare. It is an adjective describing his personality which is the selfless act of serving his children.



Rudra,Mahakaal,Bhairav,Jehovah,Holy Spirit, Elohim,..and the list goes on...
There is no end to his names.

Inspite of having many names he is most popularly called Shiv. His name never changes as the name is on his soul. So an adjective "sada" or forever is added before his name, unlike a human whose names change as they change bodies. His devotees affectionately call him using various adjectives describing his nature, leading to multiple names.

tasks of our father

When? End of the Iron age
The time when He comes to carry out his duties is called the confluence age (between End of Iron age & Beginning of Golden age). Time is cyclic and the cycle is completed at the end of every 5000 years. Each cycle consists of 4 yugs or periods namely the golden period, the silver period, the copper period and the iron period. Towards the end of the Iron age when every soul on Earth is drained out of energy, it is then that the Supreme Soul enters this material world made of 5 elements from Paramdham. This time period is described as the transition period/tranformation age/Sangam yug. This is the time for change. This is the time for a journey of transformation from Iron age towards Golden age.
What does He do?
He teaches Rajyog meditation and reminds us of our true self. We were once deities on this Earth, and we were happy, prosperous, complete and led a pure life. Nature was at her best, there was no scarecity and it was then Bharat was called Heaven, the golden age, the age of abundance. He then offers to give us back that same life of beauty and complete happiness. He comes here to create paradise once again, that is lost now.
To create Heaven again, his task is to purify the souls. The only way to cleanse the soul of its stains is through surrender of our vices or negativities to Him, and reunite with Him through meditation. For this sacrifice is needed, sacrifice of our five vices that caused us pain-lust, anger, greed, attachment, pride. He advises the souls to become soul conscious and become Brahman or mouth borne children of Brahma
The Supreme soul, our Father transforms ordinary human beings to deities. After we souls purify ourselves in the fire of yoga or meditation, we are then reborn as deities in the golden world (satyug).
How? He Needs a Human
To convey His message he needs a mouth to speak

The Supreme Soul needs to communicate with all human souls. But how? All human souls have a body and the Supreme soul does not. Hence He needs to become like us in order to talk, interact, explain and communicate.He takes the support of an ordinary human being and makes him his instrument in order to do his task. He names him Prajapita Brahma and starts his task.

why does he need a medium

The Supreme soul does not take birth and nor does he die like other souls. He only enters this Corporeal world once in a cycle to transform it. However all the souls are encased in physical outfits called bodies. In order to reach them, the Supreme soul needs to wear a physical costume as well. However since He does not come into the cycle of births and deaths, he cannot come into the womb of a mother and take birth like other souls. He enters the body of a person who is already full grown, 60 years old, experienced and has lived a life of family bliss, managed a successful business and was deeply religious.He was known as Lekhraj Kripalini. He is the same soul who was born during the Golden period as Lord Krishna. He has completed his maximum 84 births and this is his last birth in the cycle. As per this world drama this soul forgets his births and that he was the same soul who was once known as Krishna. The Supreme soul gets drawn to this soul and makes him his instrument in order to fulfill his huge duty of transforming this world into heaven once again.


He has to speak & needs a mouth

Gradually as Lekhraj Kripalini starts understanding how God needs his help to change the world, he changes his lavish lifestyle into simple and a spiritual one. He starts feeling distaste for the old world, and starts longing for the new world that the Supreme soul is planning to make. The Supreme soul uses his mouth to speak the knowledge known as "Gita gyan". Lekhraj Kripalini is renamed as Prajapita Brahma or Adidev and he is the first Brahman, and the ancestor of all human souls. In the scriptures he is shown as the old deity with white beard, four heads representing the knowledge shared by God was spread across all directions, and he is seen with four arms representing cooperation that he received from other mouth born Brahmans.

CONNECTING WITH THE SUPREME SOUL

Rajyoga Meditation

The way to connect with the Supreme soul is through meditation

Now that we know Him, how do we connect to Him? To achieve this we need to know ourselves first as only self realisation can lead to God realisation. Just as our Father is a tiny point of energy, we too are the same. We are all energy and is known by various names like soul, atma, rooh, spirit etc. Only because we are using a physical costume called "body", we lost the realisation with time that we are souls. We enter a bodily costume, use it, then discard it after use to attain another one and get into this cycle. However our Father, the Supreme Soul does not go through this. He neither takes a body nor sheds one. In order to connect with Him the first step is to realise ourself as a soul, or a tiny point of light residing in the center of the forehead that controls the entire body. Then we can slowly focus on the Supreme soul, our Real Father, whom we affectionately call "Shiv Baba", that He too is a soul but He is the Highest of High, purest and most powerful. The word "yog" means to "unite"/"add". In the process of meditation with visualization technique we unite with the Supreme soul using our mind and intellect. As he is forever charged with infinite energy, I the soul start receiving the energy in the process of union, and thus get rejuvenated, recharged and refreshed.
